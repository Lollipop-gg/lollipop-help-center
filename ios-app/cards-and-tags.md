# Cards and Tags

This page is still being documented.

Lollipop works with tappable products, cards, and tags, but the current iOS app review for this Help Center did not confirm a separate consumer-facing card or tag management screen yet.

## What is confirmed today

- Tapping a Lollipop-enabled product, card, or tag can open a claim flow in the iOS app.
- Claimed items can appear in your collection.

## What is still being expanded

- Whether the app includes a standalone area to manage cards or tags directly
- Any card- or tag-specific settings that normal users can edit in the app
