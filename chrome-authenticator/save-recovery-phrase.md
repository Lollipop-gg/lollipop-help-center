# Save your secret recovery phrase

Your secret recovery phrase is the real backup for your account.

You need it if:

- You forget your local password
- You remove the account from this browser
- You set up the Authenticator on another device
- Your browser data is deleted or lost

## Reveal and save the phrase

Click or tap the recovery phrase area to reveal the phrase, then write it down and store it somewhere private.

![Account created recovery phrase screen](../assets/chrome-authenticator/account-created-recovery-phrase.png)

The recovery phrase is hidden until you reveal it during setup.

After you have saved the phrase, check **I have written down this phrase**, then continue.

> **Warning:** Never share your secret recovery phrase with anyone. Lollipop will never ask for it.

## View the phrase later

You can view the phrase later from **Manage**, but you will need your current password.

![Recovery phrase reveal screen](../assets/chrome-authenticator/recovery-phrase-reveal.png)

The recovery phrase screen should only be opened in a private place.
